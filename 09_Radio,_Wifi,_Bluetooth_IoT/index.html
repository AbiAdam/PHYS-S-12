# Week 9: Radio, Wifi, Bluetooth Iot ## Goal: To make an LED turn on and off via Bluetooth LE. I used the [example from class](https://nathanmelenbrink.github.io/lab/networking/huzzah3.html), and the [work of a past student](https://laurenbhr.github.io/PHYSCI-70/09_bluetooth/index.html) who provided me with the app and saved me a lot of frustration with the intricacies of hex and ASCII. ## Code/Bluetooth Interface: I modified the code as specified by the tutorial, and then made it so that if you inputted the value '1' to the bluetooth, it would turn the LED on, and so that any other value would turn the LED off. I used the app recommended in the other students documentation, becasue I had tried two apps before and neither of them worked as advertised. The app is called LightBlue and I wholeheartedly recommend it.
