I mixed a 1:1 ratio of part A and part B, then stirred it until the colour was uniform, and then poured it into my mold. However, I had extra silicon and there was an Elmo carving in my block of waz so I also made an Elmo mold. I then let the mold set for 24 hours until the next lab session, although I doubt it needed that long.
